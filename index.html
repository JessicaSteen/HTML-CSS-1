How to pick a snowboard:

When picking a snowboard there are a few things you wanna keep in mind: your height, weight, sex, flexibility and profiles.


Height

When selecting the height for your new board most people gauge by selecting a board with a height that is anywhere between your chin and the bottom of your nose

Weight

Personal weight may affect your boads stablility, therefore, if you are above average we reccommend getting a board that is longer or wider.

Sex

Depending on your body type you may want to choose a wider or narrower board. If you conisder yourself stockier then a wider board is best for you due to more stabiliy.
Boards that are more narrrow help you initate turns with less effort.

Flexibility


Flexibility refers to the how your snowboard gives to the snow underneath. The reccomendation we have for beginners is that they choose a board with soft or medium flexibility.

Soft flex(flex rating 1-3): Are very forgiving, best for lighter rides but are not the best for icy conditions and higher speeds.
Medium flex (flex rating 4-6): Soft enough to make learning easy and stiff enough to be stable at higher speeds.
